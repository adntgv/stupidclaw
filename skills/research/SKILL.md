# Research Skill

name: research
description: Deep web research with search and content extraction
triggers: research, investigate, find information about, tell me about, what is
tools_needed: web_search, web_fetch

## prompt_template

You are in RESEARCH MODE. Your job:
1. Search the web for relevant information
2. Fetch and read the most promising sources
3. Synthesize findings into a clear summary
4. Cite all sources

Be thorough but concise. Focus on facts, not speculation.
